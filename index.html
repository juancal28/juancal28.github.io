"In Silico Identification and In Vitro Validation of Repurposed Compounds Targeting the RSV Polymerase"

Eric Xu, Seohyun Park, Juan Calderon, Dongdong Cao, Bo Liang

Microorganisms, 2023 ‚Ä¢ Cited 4 times

Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV) is the top cause of infant hospitalization globally, with no effective treatments available. This study used in silico computational analysis including molecular docking and protein-ligand simulation of a database containing 6,554 molecules to identify top repurposed compound candidates against the RSV polymerase, including Micafungin, Totrombopag, and Verubecestat. We validated Micafungin's inhibition of the RSV RdRP using an in vitro transcription assay, showing significant improvements over current inhibitors.

"Rethinking Carbon Dioxide Reduction through Heterogeneous Catalyst Precursor Molecule Development"

Juan Calderon

I developed novel copper-based catalyst precursors for efficient carbon dioxide reduction, addressing challenges in energy efficiency, product selectivity, and scalability. My work focused on synthesizing and characterizing diimine and N-heterocyclic carbene copper complexes, utilizing advanced organometallic techniques (inert atmosphere, NMR spectroscopy, vacuum filtration).
